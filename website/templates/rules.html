Rules

War Worlds is not a game with a lot of rules. However, in the interest of ensuring everybody is able to have as much fun as possible, you must abide by the rules set out here. Failure to do so could result in your empire being reset, or banned.

1. Access the game only from actual Android phones and tablets

That means no emulators and no scripts for automating your empire.

2. Do not make use of auto-clickers or other automation tools

It's not fair for players who are trying to play the game the way it was intended if you're using auto-clickers or auto-tappers to automate your play.

3. Bugs should be reported, not exploited

Sometimes the game has bugs. Sometimes bugs allow you to do things that give you an unfair advantage. I'm just one person, and I don't have a team of QA engineers testing the game and making sure it's bug-free. So if you find a bug, please report it to me and do not exploit it for your own benefit.

4. Do not grief or harass other players

The game is a war game, obviously, so you're expected and even encouraged to attack other players. However maliciously targeting particular players, harassment and other anti-social behavior (including in chat) are not acceptable.
